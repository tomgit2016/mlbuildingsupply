ML Building Supply Ltd. is an aluminum wholesale company selling a variety of high quality aluminum products and building materials for the commercial and residential constructional industry. Our company was established in October 2011 and now located in Delta, British Columbia Canada.

In just eight years, we have built a network of over 100 corporate clients in BC and across Canada by providing solutions in sourcing and custom fabrication of key building components for a range of complex projects and developments.

ML Building supply has established long-term relationships with leading distributors and manufacturers in Canada and China ensuring our products meet the highest standards in innovation and design. We offer a wide range of products needed for both exterior and interior projects like aluminum fence castings, screws, hinges and vinyl flooring. Additionally, our factory uses the most advanced technology and machinery for producing complex and custom bending for our customers.

Our professional staff and sales team work hard every day to assist our customers with precise recommendations, and delivery of key products and materials to locations all across Canada. We offer service in English, Mandarin, and Cantonese. Purchase orders are accepted by phone or email.
